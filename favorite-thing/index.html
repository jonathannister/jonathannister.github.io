Webpage on My Favorite Thing About School, EBC Trips!

Some things that make EBC Trips my favorite things about school include:

  • It's a break from regular school.
  • We are able to spend time with our friends in a new environment.
  • EBC is a great opportunity to make many new friends as well.
  • The activities and trips themselves are always super unique and fun.
  • It's easy to step out of your comfort zone.
