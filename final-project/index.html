About Me

I am a garden designer and coach and provide my services on an hourly basis. My rate is $50 per hour. During the initial consultation we would discuss what your project goals, scope, timeline and budget are. I also like to understand what your specific design aesthetic is, any specific problems you're trying to solve, and possible approaches. I'll share some initial thoughts on approach and we can decide on next steps.

I spent over 25 years in the tech industry, but after retiring from Microsoft in 2013 I went back to school for my real passion and earned a degree in Environmental Horticulture. A friend and I started Dig In in 2017. I have a strong belief in sustainable gardening and in preserving our limited resources. I encourage the use of native plants, where it makes reasonable sense, but also love taking advantage of the diversity of plants that thrive in our mild climate. My recommendations will come with that as a basis. My desire is to work with people who appreciate their gardens and want to know more about them. The role I prefer is Garden Coach, which is focused on teaching my clients and working with in their garden directly. Although I work to be flexible and provide the level of service that best meets your needs and interests.

Questions For You

• Do you have a specific timeframe and/or budget in mind?
• Do you have a particular style of landscape you’re interested in?
• Are there examples of neighbors yards or gardens you've viewed online that you really like or really don’t like?
• Are there plants that you love and must have – or that you hate?
• Are there specific color schemes that you find most appealing, or do not want?
• Are you looking to update your hardscape as well as your landscape?
• Do you have a particular goal with your landscape, or any specific frustrations that I should be aware of?
• Do you garden and maintain the property yourself, or do you use a landscape company? • Are you interested in learning more?
• What is your preferred method of communication (email, phone, text)?
• Are you available to meet during the weekday, evening or weekend?
