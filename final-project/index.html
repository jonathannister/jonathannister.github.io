I also like to understand what your specific design aesthetic is, any specific problems you're trying to solve, and possible approaches. I'll share some initial thoughts on approach and we can decide on next steps.

What is A Garden Coach?


Garden coaching is just like it sounds like; the goal is to help you become a better gardener and create a beautiful garden. Coaching is meant for people who already like to garden but desire some expert help to maximize their landscape. It is a very collaborative process. Note that this is different from landscapers. Rather than doing all of the gardening work, I am able to teach you how to best care for your garden, such as pointing you in the right direction and advising where to get started. Other tips include how to make a garden lower maintenance, more visually appealing, environmentally friendly, and more. For more details and points to cover before your first visit, head to the Initial Consultation page.
