About Me

My name is Tracy Botsford. I spent over 25 years in the tech industry, but after retiring from Microsoft in 2013 I went back to school for my real passion and earned a degree in Environmental Horticulture. A friend and I started Dig In in 2017. I have a strong belief in sustainable gardening and in preserving our limited resources. I encourage the use of native plants, where it makes reasonable sense, but also love taking advantage of the diversity of plants that thrive in our mild climate. My recommendations will come with that as a basis. My desire is to work with people who appreciate their gardens and want to know more about them. The role I prefer is Garden Coach, which is focused on teaching my clients and working with in their garden directly. Although I work to be flexible and provide the level of service that best meets your needs and interests.
