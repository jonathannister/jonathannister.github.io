Seattle Homelessness

Website by Jonathan, Anish, Dash, and Alan

Homelessness in Seattle is a growing problem, with 349 homeless out of every 100,000 residents. Recently, this number has increased by 23%. Continue reading, and check out the pages above, to learn more and help out.

For this project, our aim is to help out the greater Seattle community by sharing information about the homeless community in Seattle, including causes, statistics, data, and resources that you can use to help.
