Problems of Homelessness

Who is Homeless?

Homelessness is a broad term, and there are different levels of homelessness, whether it be living in a homeless shelter or living in a tent by the highway. Because of this, it's difficult for officials to determine who is homeless, and how they must solve the homeless crisis in seattle

Why Efforts From Officials Have Not Worked

Homelessness has been categorized as an issue that is deeper than just not being able to afford a home. It is caused by deeper systemic issues like racism and access to education. There is a correlation between who has access to schooling and connection to culture and society. Homelessness will remain an issue until those who have experienced homelessness have a say in what the solution (if there is a viable one) is.
