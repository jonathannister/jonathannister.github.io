Causes of Homelessness

Causes of homelessness in Seattle stem from the rise in rent, and income inequality. Infation has become a big factor of homelessness in recent months. Other factors are drug addiction and domestic violence that can occur in the Seattle area. Another big factor is unwillingness to move into homeless shelters. The Seattle government also needs to fund the homeless more than it currently does.
