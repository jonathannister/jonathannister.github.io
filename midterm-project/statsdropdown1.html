Statistics

Homelessness

Homelessness is currently Seattles biggest problem with over 13,500 homeless just last year. Costs of living have risen significantly over the past decade forcing thousands out of their homes. Additionally, the impact of Covid19 disrupted aid and voluntary efforts to help the homeless. Over the past decade, homelessness has increased by more than 30%.

Misconceptions

Many people see those who are homeless as criminals, people who consume/use illegal substances, or someone who lives on the street. While it can be true in some cases, it's usually the other way around. Those who are homeless are usually under financial stress and in some cases can live with another relative or someone they know.
